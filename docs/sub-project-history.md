| 36 | Recommender v2 — algorithmic rebalance + calibrated percentage. | 37 | Recommender v3 — math-review fixes. External math expert reviewed `algo.md` and flagged five concerns. Five corresponding patches landed: (1) **Drop μ at scoring time** — facet multiplier was being applied at both affinity-construction AND scoring (compounding to μ², so Primary subgenre matches were 36× content matches). Now applied only at construction; scoring is `score = Σ aff(t) × idf(t) × β(t,F) + B_coven`. (2) **Length penalty γ=0.5** — divide raw score by `√|tags(F)|` so heavily-tagged films don't accumulate raw advantage from breadth. (3) **Smoothed + clamped IDF** — `clamp(log(1 + N/(1+df)), 0.75, 3.0)` replaces raw `log(N/df)` to dampen extremes on small catalogs. (4) **Aversion vector** — new `getUserAversion()` parallel to own-affinity, accumulating dislike magnitude with the same time-decay + facet-multiplier + cap pattern. Scoring now subtracts `λ=0.8 × aversion-mass` from positive score, so explicitly-disliked tags actively push films down (not just zero out positive matches). (5) **Score bands replace % pill** — "94%" implied probability we couldn't validate; replaced with rank-percentile bands (Hexed for You / Strong Match / Good Omen / Strange Pull) plus a separate Coven Favorite badge. Calibration helper unwired from the display path (preserved for future use); circularity concern sidestepped because bands depend on rank, not anchored percentages. 92 unit specs pass. No schema changes. | `2026-05-02-recommender-v2-design.md` (delta documented in `algo.md`) |
